Residing in South East England, Harun Blissett aka Trimer has been heavily involved in the DnB scene for over 15 years now.

Trimer's vibe and passion for the music really comes across through his energetic and explosive DJ sets. He has played a number of venues including places such as Ministry of Sound London, Fabric London, Cable London and Let it Roll Festival Prague.
